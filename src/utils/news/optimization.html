The Optimization phase focuses on improving performance, efficiency, and overall user experience after the initial implementation has been completed.

During this stage, systems are analyzed to identify bottlenecks, reduce load times, and enhance responsiveness. Developers refine code, streamline processes, and apply best practices to ensure the application runs smoothly under different conditions.

Optimization also includes improving accessibility, scalability, and maintainability. This ensures that the system can handle growth and adapt to future requirements without compromising quality.

Continuous monitoring and testing are essential to measure improvements and detect new areas for enhancement. By iterating on performance, the final product becomes faster, more stable, and more efficient for end users.
