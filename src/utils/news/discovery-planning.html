The Discovery & Planning phase marks the foundation of every successful project. During this stage, teams work closely with stakeholders to understand goals, identify requirements, and define the overall vision.

Through research, workshops, and strategic analysis, we gather key insights that help shape the direction of the project. This ensures that every decision made later is aligned with both user needs and business objectives.

A clear roadmap is then established, outlining scope, timelines, and deliverables. By investing time in proper planning, we reduce risks, improve efficiency, and set the stage for a smooth development process.

Ultimately, Discovery & Planning is about transforming ideas into a structured and actionable plan that guides the entire project lifecycle.
