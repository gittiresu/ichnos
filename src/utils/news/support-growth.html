The Support & Growth phase focuses on maintaining the product after launch while continuously improving it based on user feedback and evolving needs.

Ongoing support ensures that any issues, bugs, or performance concerns are quickly identified and resolved. This helps maintain system stability and ensures a reliable experience for all users.

At the same time, growth strategies are implemented to expand functionality, reach new audiences, and adapt to changing market demands. This may include new features, integrations, and performance enhancements.

By combining consistent support with strategic growth, the product remains relevant, competitive, and capable of delivering long-term value.
